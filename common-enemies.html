Common Enemies

Common enemies are the baseline threats players encounter most frequently during runs. They establish the combat rhythm of each zone, teach core mechanics, and encourage careful positioning, stamina management, target prioritization, and resource control.

Baseline Humanoid Threat

Shamblers

Shamblers are slow, corrupted humanoids that attack methodically and become dangerous in groups.

  • Teach attack timing and defensive spacing.
  • Pressure the player in confined spaces.
  • Encourage stamina management and controlled positioning.
Flanker / Pressure Enemy

Skulkers

Skulkers are fast, agile enemies that attempt to flank the player and punish tunnel vision.

  • Force awareness of surroundings.
  • Create pressure when paired with Shamblers.
  • Work well in corridors, chokepoints, and ambush spaces.
Ranged Control Enemy

Spinecasters

Spinecasters attack from range using echo-infused projectiles.

  • Teach target prioritization.
  • Encourage use of cover and movement.
  • Control vertical areas, chokepoints, and static positions.
Rush / Grapple Predator

Thrash Beasts

Thrash Beasts are quadrupedal predators that rush and grapple the player.

  • Have telegraphed but dangerous attacks.
  • Break up humanoid-heavy encounters.
  • Are especially threatening in open areas where mobility matters.
Common enemies provide predictable challenges that prepare the player for higher-risk encounters such as elites, minibosses, and boss-level threats.

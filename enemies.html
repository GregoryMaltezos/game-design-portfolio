Enemy Modifiers & World Pressure

Some enemy behaviors act as modifiers rather than fixed enemy categories. These systems can appear across different encounter types to increase replay variety and reinforce the instability of the world.

  • Corruption Variants: enemies may appear in corruption-infused states, altering behavior, increasing danger, and rewarding stronger Echo progression.
  • Human / Faction Enemies: rival extractors, cultists, and scavengers create unpredictable encounters and reinforce the idea that other groups are active in the world.
  • Encounter Composition: common enemies, elites, and environmental hazards can be combined to create pressure without relying only on higher stats.
Enemies are not simply obstacles. They are the pressure that gives meaning to the player's decisions.

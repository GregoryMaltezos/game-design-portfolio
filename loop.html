Core Loop

The Core Loop represents the central player experience. Every run follows a cycle of preparation, combat, loot collection, extraction and evolution.

1. Prepare (Hub Phase)

  • Select weapons, gear and consumables.
  • Apply buffs, craft items and complete side objectives.
  • Plan strategy based on the chosen zone.

2. Enter Zone

  • Choose a general spawn area.
  • The system attempts to provide semi-safe spawns.
  • Occasionally players may spawn close to danger.

3. Fight & Explore

  • Engage AI enemies and environmental hazards.
  • Elite enemies offer better rewards.
  • Defeating powerful enemies can reveal your location.
  • Uber bosses provide guaranteed rewards at high risk.

4. Loot

  • Gather weapons, resources and consumables.
  • Loot is only secured after successful extraction.
  • Greed increases risk.

5. Extract

  • Reach extraction points.
  • Survive until extraction completes.
  • Death before extraction results in losses.

6. Upgrade

  • Develop Echoes.
  • Improve the hub.
  • Prepare for the next run.

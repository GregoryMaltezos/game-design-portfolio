Core Loop

The Core Loop represents the central player experience. Every run follows a cycle of preparation, combat, loot collection, extraction and evolution.

Prepare

  • Select weapons, gear and consumables.
  • Craft items and complete side objectives.
  • Plan strategy based on the zone.

Enter Zone

  • Choose a spawn area.
  • Semi-safe spawn locations.
  • Occasional dangerous spawns create tension.

Fight & Explore

  • Fight AI enemies.
  • Encounter elite enemies.
  • Search for valuable loot.
  • Risk revealing your position.

Loot

  • Gather resources and equipment.
  • Greed increases risk.

Extract

  • Reach extraction points.
  • Secure your progress.
  • Death before extraction means losses.

Upgrade

  • Develop Echoes.
  • Improve the hub.
  • Prepare for the next run.

Core Loop

The Core Loop represents the central player experience. Every run follows a cycle of preparation, combat, loot collection, extraction, and evolution. This loop is the backbone of gameplay, ensuring that every decision matters while reinforcing the Echo progression system.

1. Prepare (Hub Phase)

Before entering a zone, players prepare for the expedition from the hub.

  • Select loadout: weapons, gear, consumables, and quests.
  • Apply temporary buffs, craft items, or complete side objectives.
  • Plan a general strategy based on known zone characteristics.

The hub acts as a safe preparation space where players decide how much risk they are willing to take into the next run.

2. Choose Spawn Area & Enter Game

Players select a general area of the zone rather than a fixed spawn point.

  • The system attempts to spawn the player in a semi-safe location.
  • Spawn locations remain uncertain to avoid fully predictable runs.

System Malfunction Mechanic

Occasionally, the spawn system can malfunction and place players near higher-risk elements.

  • Players may spawn near enemies or hostile environmental conditions.
  • Players may spawn near enemy players who are full HP and have not been in combat recently.
  • These malfunctions create tension and reinforce the unstable nature of the world.

3. Fight & Exploration

Players explore the zone, fight AI enemies, react to environmental hazards, and search for valuable resources.

Special / Elite Enemies

  • Optional higher-tier enemies that drop better loot or unique consumables.
  • Defeating them may trigger global alerts, such as shockwaves or sounds detectable by other players.
  • This creates a tradeoff between reward value and positional exposure.

Uber Boss

  • One optional Uber Boss can appear per zone.
  • Defeating the boss grants an immediate extraction opportunity and high-tier rewards.
  • The encounter balances high risk with guaranteed reward while preserving combat tension.

4. Loot

Players collect weapons, consumables, gear, and resources during the run.

  • Loot from elites and bosses offers stronger rewards but increases the risk of continuing.
  • All collected loot remains unsecured until the player extracts successfully.
  • The more valuable the inventory becomes, the harder the extraction decision becomes.

5. Extraction

Players must navigate to an extraction zone and leave safely.

  • Successful extraction locks in Echo progression, stat upgrades, and collected loot.
  • Dying or failing extraction may result in corruption, mutations, loot loss, or other long-term consequences.
  • Extraction is the main pressure point of the run, forcing players to decide when to stop pushing forward.
“Do I leave now with what I have, or risk everything for something better?”

6. Upgrade & Hub Activities

After extraction, players return to the hub and convert the run’s outcome into long-term progression.

  • Spend resources to upgrade gear or craft items.
  • Purge, respecialize, or develop Echoes.
  • Prepare for the next run with improved strategy and knowledge.

Each completed run gives the player more information, more options, and stronger future potential.

Grigoris Maltezos

Echoes

Echoes is a dark medieval PvPvE extraction action concept where every run changes not only the player’s loot, but their future potential. The player is a failed experiment trapped between life and death: a soulless vessel that evolves by absorbing the memories and knowledge contained within weapons left by fallen warriors.

Design Pillars

High Risk / High Reward

Every run creates tension through loot, survival, extraction, and permanent consequences.

Weapon-Based Evolution

Using weapons affects the player’s form, stats, abilities, and long-term progression.

Dark Medieval Atmosphere

The world is cursed, hostile, and built around decay, experimentation, and lost identity.

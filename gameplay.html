Gameplay Systems

Echoes is built around interconnected systems where preparation, combat, loot collection, extraction, and progression all feed into each other. The goal is to make every run feel dangerous, valuable, and personally transformative.

"Do I leave now with what I have, or risk everything for something better?"

Core Loop

Prepare → Enter → Fight → Loot → Extract → Upgrade → Repeat.

Each run asks the player to balance safety, greed, progression, and survival.

Combat System

Combat is designed around timing, stamina management, positioning, dodges, parries, and commitment.

Success comes from reading enemies, managing stamina, and understanding risk rather than overwhelming power.

Echo System

Weapons contain Echoes: traces of previous owners.

Using weapons gradually transforms the player through memory, familiarity, and evolution.

Extraction Mechanics

Loot, Echo progress, and long-term growth are secured only if the player survives and extracts successfully.

Failure introduces corruption and instability, making every decision meaningful.

Progression & Upgrades

Progression is tied to loot, weapon familiarity, Echo evolution, and hub upgrades.

Players become stronger through mastery and survival rather than traditional leveling.
